Frequently Asked Questions



Q1).How soon can i get the report?

Ans:We make an effort to share the reports at the earliest possible, but definitely within 12 hours.


Q2).When will sample collection takes place?

Ans:Depending on when you make the booking, and the slot selected, the sample collection can vary from 2-4 hours to early morning of the next day.


Q3).Can Children take this test?

Ans:Children above 12 years of age can take this test. If your child is below the age of 12 years, it is best you take a pediatrician’s advice before booking the test.


Q4).Who needs Full Body Checkup?

Ans:Anyone above 25 years who has not done any full body checkup in last 6-12 months & who wants to understand their health better.


Q5).What are the conditions treated by general physicians?

Ans:General physicians treat conditions like headaches, flu, urinary infections, blood pressure, diabetes, common aches, etc.


Q6).Do general physicians offer home consultation?

Ans:Yes, most of the general physicians do offer home consultation in case of emergencies or when requested.


Q7).Will the consultation and diagnosis be covered in insurance?

Ans:Most of the conditions treated by general physicians are common. Hence, most of them are not covered by insurance. However, you can always check with your insurance company.


Q8).Do general physicians provide in-house medicines?

Ans:Yes, some of the general physicians do provide in-house medicines along with prescribing medication.


Q9).What are the consultation charges of general physicians in Dehradun?

Ans:The consultation fees of general physicians in Dehradunranges approximately from Rs. 250 to Rs. 500.
