Our Mission

ACM USS organise a free health checkup for to promote awareness among functionaries involved in Health and Hospital Management. To promote research in the field of Health Care and also promote the development of high quality checkup services and community health care.

Increase the range of health checkup services wherever there are opportunities to meet an area of customer need and demand, on financially viable basis.

Provide a safe and therapeutic environment for all patient, staff, visitors and to Increase overall satisfaction rates of patients, employees and visiting medical officers.
